For each record it is provided:
======================================

- Average values of mean and standard deviation for each measurement from the accelerometer (total acceleration) and the estimated body acceleration
- Its activity label. 
- An identifier of the subject who carried out the experiment.

Notes: 
======
- Features are normalized and bounded within [-1,1].
- Each feature vector is a row on the text file.

List of variables:
==================
"subject"
	Subject, who carried out an experiment
	Values: Number from 1 to 30

"activityLabel"
	Label for performed activity
	Values: 'WALKING', 'WALKING_UPSTAIRS', 'WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS', 'SITTING', 'STANDING', 'LAYING'	

These features contains average values of standart deviation features or mean features for time/frequency domain gravity/body signals from accelerometer for X, Y and Z axis
